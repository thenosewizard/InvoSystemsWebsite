What is Big Data?

BigData is all about business users using the open source technology to get the business Insights to solve the business problems and/or to achieve business goals. BigData is dependent on data which has 3 primary contributors like Volume (size), Velocity (speed) and Variety (data types). The data is normally classified as Structured (Databases, excel etc), Unstructured (social media, clickstream, video, audio, search etc) and Semi-structured (xml,CDR,logs etc). InvoSystems can assist customers and partners to design and setup large hadoop cluster to process large amount data in TeraBytes or PetaBytes.
